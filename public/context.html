General Context

The plots presented in this study are entirely based on the context of stock market. Specifically, the data points given in our study describes stock moving average over a period of time. Moving average shows a stock's closing price over a number of trading days. An example of moving average plot of Google over a month (from Oct 17, 2022 to Nov 17, 2022) looks like the blue line in below image (where the blue arrow points at). Note that the moving average reflects a smoother trend of stock prices, in contrast to general price of the stock (red line in the image) that is more volatile.

Our Study

In our study, we selected true data points collected from Robinhood that reflect the moving average of some stocks over approximately 3 months. However, the stocks used in our study are anonymized and you will not be shown names of the stocks. In each plot, x-axis describes time and y-axis describes closing price of the stock scaled into values between 0 and 100. Your task is to predict the future stock price.
