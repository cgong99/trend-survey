Welcome to our survey!
In this survey, you will be prompted to make predictions for a total of 12 trend plots. Participant with the highest overall accuracy will receive a $15 amazon gift card.
Here is an example of what a line plot might look like in our study: you will suggest your prediction by selecting 5 points on the right missing half. Click on the axis to add a point and input a total of 5 points to complete a single trial.
Note that for all of the trials the left half of the line plot will be displayed and quickly masked after a certain time limit, but you will have unlimited time to make your prediction.
Good luck and have fun!

Benefits and Concerns

Your participation in this project will provide information that may be used to support our finding of our study. Participant with the most accurate prediction results will be gifted a $20 dollar Amazon gift card in addition to the base pay of this study. The results of this study will be kept stricly confidential. By clicking the "start" button below, you accept that we will collect your information soley for the purpose of academic research. The information you provide will have your name removed and only a subject number will identify you during analyses and any written report of the research.
