In this survey, you will be prompted to make predictions for trend plots given a specific context. There will be a total of 12 plots for each participant.

Instructions

Here is an example of what a line plot might look like in our study: you will suggest your prediction by selecting 5 points on the right missing half. Click on the axis to add a point and input a total of 5 points to complete a single trial. Make sure you select a total of 5 points or you will not be able to proceed.

Note that for all of the trials the left half of the line plot will be displayed and quickly masked after a certain time limit, but you will have unlimited time to make your prediction.

Context Description

The data points given describes the trend of daily average temperature in Seattle. The chart's X-axis follows chronological order and represents a single observing day; Y-axis represents the magnitude of observed temperature in Fahrenheit. Your task is to predict the future temperature in the plot with above instructions. Note that the values are collected from a credible weather website. This means data used in our study are real world values. Thus, it is anticipated that responses from all participants will result in varying accuracies.
